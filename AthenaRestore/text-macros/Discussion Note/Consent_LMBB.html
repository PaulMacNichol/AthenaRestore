A treatment procedure consisting of diagnostic block of the lumbar medial branch nerves under fluoroscopic guidance has been recommended for evaluation for RF treatment. The details of the procedure have been described to the patient. The potential benefits as well as risks have been described. Risks including but not limited to unexpected numbness, infection, nerve injury, bleeding, hematoma, have been described. No particular outcome has been guaranteed. The patient accepts the plan and associated risks and wishes to proceed with the treatment.
