In right lateral decubitus position, the PMT over the lower left costal margin was identified and marked. The area was prepped with alcohol then injected with a 27 ga needle onto the periosteum of the rib and the overlying cartilage. A total of 3 ml bupivacaine 0.25% and 2 ml lidocaine 2% was injected. She tolerated it well with 100% reduction of local pain.
