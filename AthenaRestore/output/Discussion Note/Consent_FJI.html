A treatment procedure consisting of injection of corticosteroid into the lumbar facet (zygopopaseal) joints under fluoroscopic guidance has been recommended for pain relief. The details of the procedure have been described to the patient. The potential benefits as well as risks have been described. Risks including but not limited to unexpected numbness, infection, nerve injury, bleeding, hematoma, have been described. No particular outcome has been guaranteed. The patient accepts the plan and associated risks and wishes to proceed with the treatment.
